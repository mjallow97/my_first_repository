About Me

Greetings! My name is Muhammed Jallow. I currently live in Canada but am originally Gambian. I have also experienced living in other countries such as Kenya, Ivory Coast, and, Tunisia. I have Bachelors of Science degree in Biology from Queens University, but my journey has taken an exciting turn into the dynamic world of Tech.


Transition into IT

During my fourth year at university, I discovered a keen interest in the evolving tech world. It was the world of Artificial Intelligence that captivated me and fueled my desire to transition into the tech industry. My entry point was as a consultant for startups leveraging Salesforce platforms. Here, I dabbled in HTML and CSS, collaborating with front-end developers to craft captivating marketing forms on Salesforce. This experience ignited a profound interest in pursuing a career as a front-end developer.


Professional Goals

By enrolling in the DTTP program, my primary objective is to acquire the essential skills to craft visually stunning web pages. This journey serves as a big stepping stone towards my broader ambition of evolving into a proficient full-stack developer.
